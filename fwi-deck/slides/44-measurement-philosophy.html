Measurement Philosophy

Real numbers from real users, not executive estimates

Operations Analogy: Engineers use time studies to measure productivity gains. FWI adapts this for corporate via user-reported hours and stories.

Why This Approach

  • Numbers come from actual users
  • Stories provide context and credibility
  • Builds organic momentum
  • Creates internal case studies
  • Proves ROI with hard data

What We'll Capture

  • Hours saved per week
  • Number of active users
  • Qualitative feedback
  • Success stories
  • Improvement suggestions

"6-12 months from now, we can literally say: this many hours saved. Real numbers from the people using it."
