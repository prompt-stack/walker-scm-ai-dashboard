Rules 5-6: Control & Prioritization

Rule 5: Limited Submission Rights

Only department heads and certain grade levels can submit FWI requests. Not open to all employees initially.

Why This Matters

  • Quality over quantity
  • Strategic alignment
  • Prevents request flooding
  • Ensures organizational priority

Rule 6: Centralized Prioritization

Kevin and Claudell review all submissions and decide what gets built and in what order.

Why This Matters

  • Single point of accountability
  • Cross-functional visibility
  • Strategic alignment
  • Resource optimization

Evolution: As we mature, submission rights may expand. Start tight, loosen with experience.
