What Qualifies as an FWI Project?

Qualifies

Internal process automation

1-3 day build time

Clear business owner

Measurable time savings

Repetitive, predictable task

Does NOT Qualify

Customer-facing systems

3-6 week projects

No identified owner

Vague "make it better" requests

Complex integrations
