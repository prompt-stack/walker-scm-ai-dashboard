FWI Operating Rules

1 Internal Focus Only

Non-customer facing systems. Customer AI goes through regular IT queue.

2 Scope/Cost/Time Limits

Quick hitters only: 1-3 days to build. No 3-6 week projects under FWI.

3 Owner Documentation

Business owner documents usage and handles training. Not IT's job.

4 Owner Accountability

Submitter collects productivity data: hours saved, user stories.

5 Limited Submission Rights

Only department heads or certain grade levels can submit requests.

6 Centralized Prioritization

Kevin and Claudell prioritize all submissions and decide build order.
