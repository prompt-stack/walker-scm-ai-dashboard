Why Culture Comes First

Fear → Trust

People fear AI will replace them. Training shows them it's a tool to make them more valuable.

Skepticism → Adoption

When people understand AI, they find their own use cases. Push becomes pull.

Isolated → Shared

Individual experiments become organizational knowledge. We learn together.

Key insight: Organizations that train first see 3x higher adoption rates than those that deploy tools first.
