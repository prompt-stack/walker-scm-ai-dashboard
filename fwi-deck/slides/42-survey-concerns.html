Addressing Staff Concerns

What we heard and how FWI responds

Concern: AI Accuracy

"How do I know if the output is correct?"

Response: Training covers output evaluation. Human review always required for important work.

Concern: Job Security

"Will AI replace my job?"

Response: AI augments, doesn't replace. We're investing in YOU, not replacing you.

Concern: Privacy/Security

"What data is safe to use?"

Response: Training covers data governance. Clear guidelines on what NOT to input.

Concern: Learning Curve

"I don't know where to start."

Response: Structured training by cohort. Start with basics, build confidence gradually.

Pillar 1 (Training) directly addresses every top concern. Literacy reduces fear.
