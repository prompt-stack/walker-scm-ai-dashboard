FWI Build Process

From intake to deployment

1

Submit Request

Department head submits friction point via intake dashboard with clear problem statement

2

Prioritize

Kevin and Claudell review, score by impact/effort, decide build order

3

Build

Internal developer (or consultant) builds solution in 1-3 days

4

Deploy & Train

Business owner receives solution, handles team training and documentation

5

Measure

Owner collects weekly feedback: hours saved, usage, user stories

Key: Business owns the solution. IT builds it. Everyone measures it.
