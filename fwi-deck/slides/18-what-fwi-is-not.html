What FWI Is NOT

Clarity on scope and intent

FWI Is Not:

  • A replacement for employees
  • Customer-facing systems
  • 6-week IT projects
  • One-time training event
  • Technology for technology's sake
  • Open to all employees immediately
  • A vendor implementation

FWI Is:

  • Augmentation of human capability
  • Internal operations focus
  • Quick wins: 1-3 day builds
  • Ongoing literacy program
  • Business value driven
  • Phased rollout by cohort
  • Walker-owned initiative

We rejected: Sequential approach (training then agents) | IT-owned documentation | Open submission | Long projects
